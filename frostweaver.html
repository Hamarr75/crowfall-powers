Ice Weave

180 S
Ground
Instant
Weaving
30 M

Creates Stable Ice or other ice types after Free Weaving.

Stable Ice is harmless and visible only to you. It can be picked up and added automatically to your Ice Stores.

If Stable Ice is created in this way within 3M of another ice type that one will be converted to Stable Ice as well.

Weaving Ice other than Stable Ice reduces your Ice Stores by one, if creating Stable Ice , or another Ice without stores, 200 Mana is drained to create the ice. If neither cost can be paid, Ice is not woven but Mana is restored instead.

Other Ice types unlocked by Free Weaving:
Cool Ice: benefits allies within 10M restoring their native resource over time.

Frigid Ice: harms enemies within 5M over time Dazing them and inflicting 100 damage. Enemies destroy it on contact taking 190 damag, becoming Rooted and suffering Ice Buildup. Tripping it again while afflicted with Ice Buildup causes Knockdown.

Refreshing Ice: quickly bursts healing allies within 10M for 47 healing.

Volatile Ice: quickly explodes damaging enemies within 5M for 100 damage.

Your Ice Storage Capacity determines the number of Ice you may have active simultaneously.

Frost Armor

180 S
Reticle
Instant
Weaving
30 M

Uses weaving to create Frost Armor wich can last for 180 seconds.

Frost Armor can be picked up by allies and you to add 3500 Armor. It lasts until you zone or until you take damage and then up to 15 seconds after the damage. Each hit adds one Frost Armor Crack counter (one per 1/2 second max) and the effect ends if you are hit after your third Frost Armor Crack.

Weaving reduces your Ice Stores by one. If your ice stores are empty, 200 Mana is drained to create the Ice. If neither cost can be paid, Ice is not woven but Mana is restored instead.

Your Ice Storage Capacity determines the number of Ice you may have active simultaneously.
